About me

ACLU Summer Program

This summer I will be attending a program at the ACLU in Washington DC

This summer I will be joining about 180 kids from around the country in an ACLU program in Washington DC. We will be learning about civil advocacy and participate in lectures and seminars as well as meet with congressmen and women about specific issues.

Paris Trip

This spring break my family and I took a trip to Paris

This Spring break my family and I went on a 10 day vacation to Paris, France. While in Paris we saw many of the famous sights like the Eiffel Tower, The Louvre, and we were lucky enough to see Notre Dame before it tragically burned down. We also had some amazing food from many different restaurants.

Alabama Immersion

This winter I traveled to Alabama with some other kids in my school as part of a program that the school offers.

Our school offers a program called immersion. In this program we spend two weeks before winter break immersing ourselves in a subject that we select from the school Immersion fair. My immersion traveled to Alabama where we went to 16th street baptist church, the civil rights museum, MLK's house, the Edmund Pettis bridge and many more places. While in Alabama we learned about the civil rights movement as well as the reprocussions that we still face today.
